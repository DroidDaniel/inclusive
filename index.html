Our Work

We specialise in building data-driven 360-degree election campaigns, offering strategic advice to the client and supporting on-ground implementation of strategies. Inclusive Minds has six internal verticals that work together to deliver cutting-edge political consulting services to the INC.

DATA ANALYTICS

The team builds models to identify and forecast voting patterns, tracks the performance of political leaders at all levels, and analyses media and social media trends for clients.

Growth and Performance Marketing

The team identifies, develops and optimises marketing strategies and channels to drive organisational growth.

Political Strategy

The ground intelligence team tracks the political pulse of the electorate. Through evidence-based research, the team proposes and shapes winning electoral strategies in collaboration with the client.

Strategic Research

The research team analyses and tracks government performance, identifies policy and electoral issues and proposes sound recommendations using a mix of policy and political research methods.

Communications

The team creates and delivers high-impact content to disseminate and amplify client messaging across print and digital media platforms. The team is also responsible for analysing and interpreting important news across multiple platforms in order to develop clear actionables for clients.

Campaigns and Operations

The team creates integrated campaigns focused on strategic issues. It specialises in brand management and supports the end-to-end implementation of on-ground campaigns, including measuring outcomes.
